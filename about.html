Personal Profile

I have significant experience in research and writing, and excel at compiling political, economic and historical information from multiple sources into clear and concise analytical reports. I am skilled in planning and leading projects both individually and as part of a team, and actively encourage my peers and coworkers to contribute their own perspectives and talents while problem-solving.
