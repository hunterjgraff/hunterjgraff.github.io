This is a concept map outlining a new cycling race spanning the North American continent. Like other legendary races in Spain, Italy and France, the North American Grand Tour would stretch over a three-week period across a diverse set of terrain. The amount of ground covered, however, would make it unique; beginning in Montreal, the tour would follow the Great Lakes into the Appalachians and conclude along the arid mountains of central Mexico.
